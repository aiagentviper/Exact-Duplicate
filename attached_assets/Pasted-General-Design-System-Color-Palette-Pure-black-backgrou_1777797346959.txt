General Design System:

Color Palette: Pure black background (#000000), white text, and dark-gray accents (zinc-900).
Aesthetic: High-contrast, editorial, luxury brand feel.
Images: All images must be grayscale (grayscale), high-contrast, and have rounded corners (rounded-2xl).
Typography: Use a clean sans-serif (like Inter). Bold headings, small muted body text.
Max Width: The content must be contained in a narrow, centered wrapper (max-w-5xl or max-w-6xl).
Please build the following sections in order:

1. Hero Section (Split Layout):

Left side: Large heading 'Meet Meily', a short bio, and a group of pill-shaped tags (Product Design, Branding, etc.). Below that, a 3-column 'Experience' table with light borders.
Right side: A large vertical grayscale image with rounded corners.
2. Recent Works (Horizontal Scroll):

A heading 'Recent Works' with a down-arrow icon.
A horizontal container of grayscale product images. Each image MUST have the 'View Casestudy' glassmorphism pill-button (semi-transparent white, backdrop-blur) at the bottom.
3. Process Section (Vertical Layout):

Left: A very tall, thin vertical image.
Right: Heading 'Process', subtitle, and two buttons ('Book a Free Call' in black, 'See Projects' in outline).
Cards: Below the text, three stacked dark cards (bg-zinc-900/50) with small icons, a numbered title (1, 2, 3), and descriptions.
4. Services Section:

Layout: Header on the left, horizontal image on the right.
Grid: A 2x2 grid of service cards (Brand Identity, Brand Design, etc.). Each card has a small icon and a border top/bottom.
Marquee: Below the grid, two 'moving ticker' lines of text/tags scrolling horizontally in opposite directions.
5. Client Reviews Section:

Top: A large horizontal image of the designer at work on the left; Title/Text/Buttons on the right.
Testimonial Cards: A horizontal row of cards. Each card has a small profile photo, Name, Role, a text quote, and 5-star icons.
Stats Bar: A 3-column dark section showing '180+ projects', '96% satisfaction', and '15+ years'.
6. FAQ (Answers) Section:

Layout: Heading and a grayscale image of a cut fruit on the left.
Right: A clean vertical Accordion list. Each item is a question with a '+' icon on the right, separated by thin borders.
Instructions for the code:

Use flex and grid for all layouts.
Use backdrop-blur-md for all glassmorphism effects.
Ensure the layout is centered and doesn't stretch to the full edges of the screen.
Make it fully responsive (stacks on mobile)."
